Smoked Macroni

Recipe by chef Rida Aftab

Ingredients

  • Boiled macaroni 1 packet
  • Boiled chicken ½ kg
  • Sausages 6
  • Oil 3 tbsp
  • Chopped ginger garlic 1 tsp
  • Chicken cube 1
  • Crushed red chilies 1 tsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Black pepper 1 tsp
  • Finely chopped carrots 1 cup
  • Cabbage 1 cup
  • Capsicum
  • Cream 1 packe
  • Coal 1 piece

Cooking Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger garlic. When aroma comes then adds ½ cup water and allow simmering.
  2. Now add chicken cube, when it dissolve in water add crushed red chilies, salt and black pepper.
  3. Then add carrots, capsicum, cabbage, sausages, boiled chicken and boiled macaroni and turn off the stove.
  4. Then mix cream and give smoke of the coal. Dish out and serve.
